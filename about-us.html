Our Company

We offer a wide range of quality products, at competitive prices all under one roof with service of a high standard.

Hardware Complete Pvt ltd is a Zimbabwean registered company, under companies Act, Chapter 24:03. The company was established over 20 years ago as a hardware equipment supplier. Supporting our customers with renovation and building project requirements is our main priority

Our Vision

To be recognized locally as a leader in the hardware business by providing customized support, where our core culture, people, innovation & ethical values represent a strong philosophy toward exceeding customer expectations.


Our Mission

To offer quality hardware products in a customer friendly shopping environment ensuring that Hardware Complete is the preferred choice in this sector
